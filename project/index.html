‘Why is US President Biden silent on China border aggression?’

US president Joe Biden’s description of India’s response to Russia vis-a-vis Ukraine as “shaky” received pushback from Indian analysts, some of whom have been pointing out that the US and its European allies continue energy purchases from Russia (by one account, up to $600 million per day), while bearing down on India. 8 charred to death: What led to carnage in West Bengal's Rampurhat

Eight people were charred to death in a late-night fire at Bogtui village in Rampurhat on Monday a couple of hours after a Trinamool Congress panchayat deputy head was killed by bike-borne miscreants. The deaths, the highest toll in a single incident reported in Bengal since the nine deaths in West Midnapore's Netai on January 7, 2011, sparked a political blame game.
